Don't be just studious, be smart!

10 tips to make an all-rounder student

1. While it's perfectly ok to have a favorite subject. Just see that other subjects don't suffer.

2. You don't have to say no to friends, internet, tv and playing sports Just set your priorities right.

3. Take good care of your health because learning becomes easier with a healthy body and mind.

4. Don't just memorize something instead try to understand it because you may forget what you've memorized, but you'll never forget what you've understood.

5.You'll never have more than 60 minutes in an hour, more than 24 hours in a day and more than 7 days in a week. So plan your study schedule smartly.

6.Avoid junk food and colas because they can turn you lethargic and overweight.

7.Don't be over confident about your memory. Make a list of assignment, test and homework, and keep it somewhere where you can see it daily.

8.Cultivate a habit of reading. Not just text books, but books in general becaus reading can ignite your mind as well improve your language skills.

9.Dozing off in class will turn you into a laughing stock. So aim to get at least 6-8 hours of sleep every night.

10.While this may seem funny, always take a shower before going to school, because a fresh body will lead to a fresh mind.
